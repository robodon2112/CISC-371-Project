Support Staff Page

This will be the page for Support staff to log into to acces and open tickets if they are experincing any sort of difficulty. There will be a "create ticket" button that will allow them to write what issues they are ecperiencing and will be sent with their user data to the MySql database I set up with a primary key (ticket #) that will be auto generated by MySql.

These tickets will be shared accorss the other users (helpdesk, admin, manager,) and each users own open and closed tickets will be available on their own log in screens.

We as a group can dicuss the nuances and features we would like to add to make the app function.
